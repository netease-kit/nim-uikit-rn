## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Conversation Long-Press Actions

The app SHALL keep the conversation long-press menu aligned with the expected action scope.

#### Scenario: Long-pressing a conversation row

- **WHEN** the user long-presses a conversation row
- **THEN** the app MUST show the stick-top toggle action
- **AND** the app MUST show the delete-conversation action
- **AND** the app MUST NOT include unrelated extra actions in that long-press menu
